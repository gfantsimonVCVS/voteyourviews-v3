### Reproductive Rights
1. "Forcing anyone, at any age — including victims of rape or incest — to give birth should not be Texas law."
2. "The difficult decision to end a pregnancy in the first trimester should be between a woman and her doctor — not the government."
3. "A doctor should not be prosecuted when a patient's life requires the termination of a pregnancy."
4. "Whether you support abortion rights or oppose them, candidates should not be elected on this issue alone."

---

### Gun Policy
1. "A gun owner should be able to buy and use a military-style weapon without government restriction."
2. "Mass shootings aren't the responsibility of gun owners, but the responsibility of untreated mental illness."
3. "It makes sense that an 18 year old can legally buy an AR-15 but cannot buy a handgun until they turn 21."
4. "Texas should continue to reject Red Flag laws based on warning signs — until a crime is actually committed, no one should have their guns taken away."

---

### Public Education
1. "Tax dollars should not be used to pay for private school tuition — it takes money away from the public schools that serve the majority of Texas children."
2. "Texas teachers earn thousands less than the national average — and every year we lose more of them because they can no longer afford to stay in the classroom."
3. "It's unacceptable that Texas found $11 billion for border security and $1 billion for private school vouchers, but refuses to fully fund the public schools educating 5 million Texas children."
4. "Our children's science education should not depend on whether their teacher puts biblical or political beliefs before scientific facts."

## DRAFTS IN PROGRESS

---

### Immigration & Border (FAR RIGHT voice)
1. "Texas taxpayers should not be forced to pay for the healthcare, education, and housing of people who entered this country illegally."
2. "Every criminal illegal immigrant who should have been deported is a preventable crime against an American citizen."
3. "If you want to live in America, get in line and do it legally — just like millions of others did."
4. "The federal government has failed to secure the border, so Texas has every right to do it themselves."

---

### Election Integrity (LEFT voice)
1. "Closing polling locations in minority neighborhoods and making lines longer is voter suppression — plain and simple."
2. "Requiring a photo ID to vote sounds reasonable until you realize hundreds of thousands of eligible Texans don't have one and can't easily get one."
3. "When Texas purges voter rolls and removes eligible voters weeks before an election, democracy is being rigged — just not in the way they claim."
4. "Every eligible Texan should be able to vote without having to fight through obstacles designed to make it harder."

---

### Climate & Energy (RIGHT voice)
1. "Texas's oil and gas industry supports millions of jobs and families — radical climate policies would destroy them overnight."
2. "The federal government has no business telling Texas how to manage its energy industry."
3. "Wind and solar sound great until the grid fails in February and people freeze to death — Texas needs reliable energy, not green experiments."
4. "Climate change policies cost real Texans real money — higher energy bills, lost jobs, and a weaker economy — for problems we didn't create."

---

### Social Security & Medicare (LEFT voice)
1. "Texans spent their entire working lives paying into Social Security and Medicare — any politician who cuts those benefits is stealing from them."
2. "Privatizing Social Security means gambling the retirement security of millions of Texans on the stock market."
3. "4.7 million Texans depend on Social Security and Medicare — cutting these programs to pay for tax cuts for the wealthy is unacceptable."
4. "No senior in Texas should have to choose between buying groceries and buying their medication."

---

### Housing & Affordability (LEFT voice)
1. "When a teacher, a nurse, or a firefighter can't afford to live in the community they serve, something is broken."
2. "Texas home prices rose over 50% in three years — and wages didn't. That's not a market working, that's a crisis."
3. "Corporate landlords buying up Texas homes and turning them into rentals is making it impossible for working families to ever own a home."
4. "A family that has lived in their Texas community for generations should not be priced out of it by out-of-state investors."

---

### Healthcare (RIGHT voice)
1. "The government has no business getting between a patient and their doctor — and that includes forcing Americans into a one-size-fits-all healthcare system."
2. "Expanding Medicaid in Texas means billions in new government spending and more dependency — not better care."
3. "Free-market competition — not government programs — is what drives down costs and improves quality in healthcare, just like in every other industry."
4. "Texas has the highest uninsured rate in the nation because Washington keeps blocking the market solutions that would actually make coverage affordable."
